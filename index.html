Our Story

About Summit

Who We Are

Passionate About
Every Vehicle

Summit Motor Co was established to bring a refreshingly personal approach to the used car market. We believe every vehicle has a story and every buyer deserves the time to find the right one.

From our premises in Holmfirth, West Yorkshire, we handpick a carefully curated selection of pre-owned vehicles — from practical everyday cars to high-performance models.

By appointment only — every customer receives our complete, undivided attention. No rushing, no pressure.
